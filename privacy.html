TL;DR
• 🧠 We don’t collect, track, or store any personal data ourselves.
• 📧 The email you enter to start a trial is stored by ExtPay.

1. What We (Don’t) Collect
The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.
To use the extension, you must start a free trial by entering your email address, which is securely stored by ExtPay, a third-party service managing trial and subscription access.

If you choose to subscribe, payments are processed via Stripe, a certified payment provider.
Neither we nor ExtPay ever access or store your payment details.

2. Data Processors
We rely on the following third parties:
• ExtPay – stores your email address to manage trial and subscription access
• Stripe – processes all payments

3. Legal Basis for Processing
When you start a trial or subscription, you give explicit consent to process your personal data.
We do not process any personal data before that point.

4. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
• Access the data held about you by ExtPay or Stripe
• Withdraw your consent at any time
• Request deletion of your personal data
• File a complaint with a data protection authority

If you withdraw consent or request deletion of your data, your access to the extension will end immediately, as we will have no mechanism to manage your subscription.
No refunds will be issued for any remaining subscription period.
We recommend exercising these rights near the end of your billing cycle.

5. Data Retention
We do not store any data ourselves.
ExtPay retains your email address until you request its deletion.
Stripe retains payment-related data as required by financial laws.

6. Children
This extension is not intended for users under the age of 16.
We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.
